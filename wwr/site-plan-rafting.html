Overview

Purpose

Here in Cool Rapids our main purpose is to get fun to families and people that likes intensive adventures, but to also have something for the not so adventurous. In order to accomplish this, we want to show our customers that whitewater rafting can be a lot of fun, but most importantly safe. We would love to show our future clients what we can offer as a first option for vacations, retreats and many more activities. With help of this site we will help you understand that safty is our first and last thing to do aswell as the different experience levels of each adventure.

Audience

Here in Cool Rapids we want to treat everyone like family, and that is why we expect lots of people to come to have some adventure. The audience for this site is from 20 - 80 people. It is pointed to adventurous people wanting to really try something new and exciting. While security is our priority, we want to let know that our customers have to have in good health and ofcourse pass all the security and health check ups. In case that we want to expierence this with our kids (we encourage this very much) minors will need to be accompanied by an adult at all times.
